About

Hi! I’m MoonMoon and I’m excited that you found your way here. I’ll be your guide to navigate the waters of living in touch with nature, others, and whatever got us here (I like to think it is “Source”).
Together, we can raise our vibration with love and creation. I'll show you how and join you on your journey to find clarity and harness the source within. We can help ourselves, and others, to stop and smell the roses...then make an oil blend out of them after thanking Source for the gift.

Services

Welcome to the Pop (aka: Parea of Power)

Our community to affirm, encourage, and educate

What you'll find here

  • Compassion- I do my best to avoid gendered content or cultural appropriation from paths I cannot understand the inextricable historical implications of the practice
  • General terminology - gender and path neutral
  • Source- my word for the universe, a god, all the gods, the energy that is the impetus for life in all of us
  • spiritual/pagan - you won't find Wicca specific things here, but my more general style requires individualization and you can make it as religious as you would like
  • Community for solo practitionors to lift each other up
  • Tricks and Treats to make your magic work for you

What to Expect

Eclectic practices and rituals with the utmost respect and honoring traditions associated with the ritual, while also customizing it to make it work best for you. You don’t need anything to be magick. You just ARE. Just the way you are. You are as magick as the stars on an isolated beach because you were brought into the universe that way. There are certain tips/ tricks of the trade to make your intentions manifest better or faster though and we will share those in our community of Pop (Parea of Power).

My Journey to Spirituality

Didn't we all end up here because we woke up? We became disillusioned with the world or our lives and realized it didn’t have to be that way? We have the power to change. I found that power slowly, but the better I understood living intentionally, the more power, I found, I had to change my surroundings. The universe is full of power, we just need to guide it.

How I practice Magic

I am a firm believer in that all you need for magic is you and your ability to manifest an intention. However you go about manifesting that intention is your own path. I like to use bath rituals with luxurious baths and burning candles with sigils carved into them while meditating on my intention. That is just my cup of tea though. If you just throw salt along your threshold and call it a day, I am down with that too. But if you are here looking for ways to speed up or amplify your practice, I can help with that. Or maybe you are just looking for inspiration on how to mix up your altar or looking for an extra magical way to make that salt you threw down work. Our community is here to support, educate, and celebrate you.
